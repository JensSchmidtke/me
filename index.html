Language, Data, and Learning

I am an applied linguist working at the intersection of language, data analysis, learning, and assessment. My work spans the analysis of behavioral data in language processing and the development, structuring, and evaluation of learning materials. In applied and institutional contexts, I use data-informed approaches to support language teaching, assessment, and training.
